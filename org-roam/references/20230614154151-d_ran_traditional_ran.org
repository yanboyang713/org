* What is D-RAN ( traditional RAN) ?
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       b273920a-66be-4ba3-8e9d-3d7fc23ba4b4
:END:
D-RAN stands for “Distributed RAN”

The diagram below shows distributed RAN. The [[id:7b67ceaf-68b1-4916-ab4f-2b95605dd813][Radio Unit (RU)]] and [[id:1329f1c2-a4a3-456b-b114-2220d3e990db][BBU]] are co-located at every cell site. Each cell site with all its radio functions are distributed and connected back to the core network through backhaul.
