#+title: drawbacks of microservices
#+filetags:  

There are, however, a few drawbacks of [[id:10682b04-b1f4-4200-a3a6-23a5d82c2795][microservices]]. These include the following:
+ Too many microservices overload the development process
+ You have to design interaction protocols
+ They can be expensive for small teams
