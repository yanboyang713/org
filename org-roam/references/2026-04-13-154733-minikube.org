#+title: minikube
#+date: 2026-04-13

Minikube lets you quickly setup a local [[id:b60301a4-574f-43ee-a864-15f5793ea990][Kubernetes]] cluster. It supports the use of Virtual Machines, containers and even bare metal as its driver.
