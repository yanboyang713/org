#+title: attention head
#+date: 2026-06-20

An attention head is the core functional unit inside a [[id:6ec03dde-9090-462b-b127-7fa998be09db][Transformer]]'s multi-head attention layer. It acts as a specialized data-retrieval system that determines how much "importance" or focus one token in a sequence (e.g., a word) should place on other tokens, capturing specific syntactic or semantic relationships.
