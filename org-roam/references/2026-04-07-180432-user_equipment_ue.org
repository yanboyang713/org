#+title: User Equipment (UE)
#+date: 2026-04-07

User Equipment (UE) refers to any device that connects to the RAN (Radio Access Network) to access mobile services provided by the network operator.

In practice, UE compatibility depends on modem firmware, supported bands, SIM or USIM configuration, and the exact core-network and RAN settings used in the lab.
